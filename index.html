About Me

I'm a 22-year-old graphic design student with a desire to become a tattoo artist. From a young age, I fell in love with art and began refining my creative skills through graphic design. Now, I'm on a mission to bridge my design expertise with the world of tattooing, using my foundation in visual aesthetics and storytelling to create meaningful and visually captivating tattoos. My dream is to leave my mark on the world, one beautiful piece at a time, and I invite you to join me on this artistic journey as I pursue my passion for ink and self-expression.

Hobbies

I enjoy a variety of hobbies, including drawing, hiking, photography, and crocheting. These hobbies offer a perfect blend of creativity, nature, and relaxation in my life.

Goals

My goal is to become a tattoo artist, a dream I've had since I first fell in love with the art form. The idea of using ink to tell stories, evoke emotions, and create lasting connections with people through my work is what fuels my passion. I'm committed to mastering the craft, learning diverse styles and techniques, and ultimately leaving my artistic mark on the world, one tattoo at a time.
